Grand Total —
  1. Total daily views = platforms × views_per_platform
  2. Daily sign-ups (own & each T2) = total_daily_views × (conversion_rate / 100)
  3. Monthly sign-ups = daily_sign_ups × days_per_month
  4. Revenue per own customer / mo = T1_commission × avg_monthly_payments
  5. Revenue per T2 customer / mo = T1_override × avg_monthly_payments
    Average monthly payments multiplies every commission — doubling it doubles all earnings.
  6. Own monthly earnings = monthly_own_signups × revenue_per_own_customer
  7. Override from one T2 = monthly_T2_signups × revenue_per_T2_customer
  8. Total T2 override = override_per_T2 × number_of_T2
  9. Grand total = own_earnings + total_T2_override
